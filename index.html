Dr. Mesfin Mekonnen

Assistant Professor

Research Areas

Dr. Mesfin Mekonnen's research focuses on understanding the interactions between human and natural systems to ensure the sustainability of freshwater resources. His work explores spatial and temporal patterns of global water footprints and their impact on ecosystems and human water security. The research aims to provide insights into sustainable water use practices and policy recommendations to address global water challenges.

Education

M.S., Environmental Science and Technology, UNESCO-IHE Institute, The Netherlands, 2005
